About!

OnlineKing24 SMP is a popular Minecraft Bedrock Server that offers a unique multiplayer experience for players around the world. The server is run by a dedicated team of experienced administrators who are committed to providing a safe, fun, and engaging environment for players to explore, build, and interact with one another.

One of the key features of OnlineKing24 SMP is its vibrant and welcoming community. The server has a friendly and supportive player base, who are always willing to help newcomers and offer guidance on gameplay and server rules

Our server has been running since May 31st, 2021, and the most important fact about our SMP is that its second name is Teddy SMP, provided by our Co-Owner. Our SMP has 115 members and 5 staffs

Overall, OnlineKing24 SMP is a fantastic Minecraft Bedrock Server that offers a dynamic and engaging multiplayer experience for players of all skill levels. Its welcoming community, range of game modes, and unique features make it a must-try for anyone looking for a new Minecraft server to join.
